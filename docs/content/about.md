# About Rogue Reads

Rogue Reads is the book-audit shelf attached to [Marika Olson Consulting](https://marikaolson.com). Each audit takes one finance, business, or systems book and runs it through a fixed extraction format: strip the persona, surface the load-bearing mechanic, separate what aged well from what aged poorly, name what the book ignores, and give an honest verdict on who should read it.

Every audit closes with **Hearth's verdict** — a one-line read from the cat that lives on the desk. Tonal range: 
-*would-knock-off-the-desk* (actively fight anyone that suggests this book)
- *hiss-worthy* (tell off anyone who suggests this book) 
- *nap-worthy* (not worth a fight, but not worth an in-depth read) 
- *windowsill-approved* (Highest honors, Hearth would deign to sleep on it, and they have standards.)

## What this isn't

- Stock tips, market timing, or get-rich-quick patterns.
- Affiliate-bait disguised as criticism. (Bookshop.org or Thriftbooks links may appear; revenue isn't the point.)
- Sponsored content. Ever.

## What this is

- Editorial book audits, written by me, Marika Olson, with the help of my AI cat.
- Closed to outside-author submissions — the voice integrity is the point. If you want to write audits, write them on your own platform; we'll happily link to good ones.
- Free to read, forever. Content is licensed CC BY-NC-SA 4.0. Thou shalt not make money off of my book audits.

## Why "Rogue"

Marika started off as Rogue Bureaucrat, when USAID was illegally destroyed over a weekend. [Rogue Bureaucrat](https://roguebureaucrat.com) is where Marika vents her political commentary. *Rogue Reads,* then, captured that same fury and frustration when she launched her business consulting [Marika Olson Consulting](https://marikaolson.com) and realized the majority of the core texts are focused on the same kind of narrative, and the same audience, as the DOGE-bros who destroyed development. Rogue Reads is intended to cut that cycle of bros-only exclusion, and make financial and business skills, and eventual financial independence, accessible to all. 

## The skill suite

The audit skill used to walk through Marika's thoughts on the book and create a unified, streamlined review runs on the [FI-skill-suite](https://github.com/XerafinaTaleSedrin/FI-skill-suite) — a free, open-source set of Claude Code skills for navigating financial independence. Anyone can clone the suite, run the audit skill against a book they've read, and produce their own audit on their own platform. Marika created the skill suite in an attempt to further break down the bros-only barriers. No post a comment for the link, no only $99.99 for my special claude prompt. It's all there, for free, for anyone to clone and use, forever, again, on a Creative Commons BY-NC-SA 4.0 license.
